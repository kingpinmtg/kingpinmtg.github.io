Rules

  • 30 starting life
  • 75 card deck, including the Commander
  • Decklist may contain only 1 copy of any card except basic lands
  • A deck's Commander may be any legendary creature, or Planeswalker
  • Commanders will begin each game in the command zone and will cost (2) more to cast for each time they have been cast from the command zone
  • All cards in a deck will be within the color identity of the deck's commander
  • No sideboard
  • If a player takes 20* total combat damage from any team's commanders, their team loses the game.**
  • If teams consist of two or more players, the starting life total is 40

*This value is subject to change pending further testing.
**All commanders on one team contribute to the 20 damage towards another team together.
