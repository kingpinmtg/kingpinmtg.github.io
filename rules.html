Rules

  • 30 starting life.
  • 75 card deck, including the Commander.
  • Decklist may contain only 1 copy of any card except basic lands.
  • A deck's Commander may be any legendary creature, or Planeswalker.
  • Commanders will begin each game in the command zone and will cost more to cast for each time they have been cast from the command zone.
  • A deck may only play cards that could be successfully cast, and its abilities activated, within your commander's color identity. (For example, if your commander's color identity is , you cannot include a card who's casting cost is , but has an activated ability cost of . You can, however, include a card whose casting cost is , since it is capable of being cast using . You may also include a card whose casiting cost is with an activated ability of since both are capable of being cast using .)
  • No sideboard
  • If a team takes 16* total combat damage from any commanders, their team loses the game. (For example, if Player A has taken 8 combat damage from Player B's commander and 4 combat damage from Player C's commander, Player A has taken 12 commander damage.)
  • A team may consist of one or more players.
  • If teams consist of two or more players, the starting life total is 40.

*This value is subject to change pending further testing.
