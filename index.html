Welcome!

Basic Overview

Kingpin is a singleton format designed to be played by 3-5 players. Decks consist of 75 cards. A legendary creature or planeswalker leads the deck of cards in its color identity from all throughout Magic's history. Starting life totals are 30.

Background

Kingpin was conceived as a form of compromise between the Oathbreaker and Commander formats. In our experience, we found that commander games took too long, and that Oathbreaker's 'Planeswalkers' only rule stifled diversity a bit too much. So we decided to try our hand at creating a new format that was had the best elements of both!
