Banlist

We decided to start with a minimal banlist to "test the waters" with deckbuilding. Only the cards that we collectively agreed were harmful to the overall health of the format were banned to start. All bans are meant to maximize deck diversity. All cards that could potentially be harmful to the format, but have not been banned are placed on the "Watchlist". Further information can be learned from the watch list page.
